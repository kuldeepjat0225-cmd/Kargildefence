About Our Academy

Shaping Tomorrow's Military Leaders

Kargil Defence Academy is dedicated to addressing the critical shortage of officers in the Indian Armed Forces by providing top-tier guidance, systematic academic coverage, and robust physical conditioning.

Our Phagi branch brings customized learning models directly to competitive candidates in the Jaipur district, bridging the rural-urban training gap seamlessly.

  • Experienced Ex-Armed Forces Mentors
  • Rigorous Written & SSB Interview Frameworks
  • Complete Physical Fitness Ground Training
  • Personality Development & English Communication

Our Specialized Programs

NDA Course

Comprehensive preparation tracking UPSC syllabus (Mathematics & General Ability Test) alongside preliminary physical conditioning for 10+2 students.

CDS / AFCAT

Tailored batches focusing on advanced aptitude, static GK, and defense specific strategies for graduates looking to join IMA, OTA, or AFA.

SSB Training

Interactive screening, psychology analysis, and GTO practice modules orchestrated by highly decorated panel reviewers.

Air Force / Navy (Solder Cadres)

Dedicated fast-track coaching targeting written and physical agility requirements for multi-stream technical and non-technical recruitment exams.
